About this project

I chose this theme because it was my favorite childhood show. I recently showed the show to my little sister and realized how much joy it brought me. The Carebears' message of kindness and caring resonates with me deeply, and I wanted to create a website that would kind of showcase this.

Design choices

I wanted to design this website to be minimal and easy to navigate, while still being visually appealing and informative. I sort of modeled the home page after pokemon playing cards with the way you have different "cards" laid out, an image in the center, and a quick description at the bottom. The details page was designed to be a bit more detailed but still trying to keep words to a minimum.

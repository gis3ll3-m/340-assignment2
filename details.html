Grumpy Bear

Grumpy Bear is known for being grumpy and always trying to find the negative in situations. Despite his grumpiness, Grumpy Bear cares deeply about his friends and will do anything to help them when they are in need

  • Belly Badge: Rain Cloud with hearts and raindrops
  • Special Ability: Creates storm clouds, produces rain, and sometimes lightning.
  • Favorite Food: Bumbleberries
  • FUN FACT: Grumpy bear is one of the ten original Care Bears created in 1982!.
